Interest Rate Spreads

This graph shows the historic interest rate spread between the Swedish Riksbank's rate and the advertised 3-month adjustable-rate mortgages offered by Swedish banks. Theoretically this shows how much money the banks could make on these mortgages. Note that the actual rates that customers pay should be lower than the advertised rates, as the banks offer discounts.

Sources

  • Riksbanken: Policy Rates
  • SHB: Bolåneräntor
  • SEB: Genomsnittsräntor för bolån - historik . Note that these are ACTUAL rates, not advertised rates. In the graph I added the difference between the actual and advertised rates as of 20241219 to all the advertised rates, to get a comparison with the other commercial banks.
  • SBAB: Bolåneräntor . Note that SBAB state that they do not offer discounts on their rates, so the advertised rates are the actual rates.
